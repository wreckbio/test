faq

Questions people actually ask

Is wreck.bio actually free?

Yeah. You can build and share your page without paying anything. Premium is optional — it unlocks video backgrounds, extra effects, bigger uploads, and a badge. No credit card needed to start.

How is this different from Linktree?

Linktree is fine if you want a plain list of buttons. wreck.bio is built for people who care about how their page looks — backgrounds, effects, discord integration, widgets, file hosting. Less corporate, more you.

Do I need to connect my Discord?

Only if you want the presence widget showing your status and avatar. Everything else works with just usernames or links you paste in.

Can I use a custom domain later?

Yep. Point your domain at your page whenever you're ready. Works fine on the free wreck.bio URL until then.

What about file hosting?

Upload images and short videos, get a direct link. Free tier gets 50MB per file, premium gets 500MB. We scan uploads and take down anything that breaks the rules.

Is my data sold?

No. We don't run ads on your page and we don't sell visitor data. Analytics are for you to see your own traffic, not for us to monetize.
